## About Us

BlockChain MUST is dedicated to providing students with hands-on experience and knowledge in blockchain technology. We aim to create a collaborative environment where students, enthusiasts, and researchers can:

* Learn about blockchain and decentralized technologies
* Develop projects and prototypes
* Participate in hackathons and competitions
* Contribute to open-source blockchain initiatives

---

## Mission

To educate, empower, and inspire Mbarara University students to explore blockchain technology and its applications, fostering innovation and practical problem-solving skills.

---

## Vision

To be the leading student blockchain hub in Uganda, recognized for pioneering blockchain research, projects, and community impact.

---

## Objectives

* Build a strong understanding of blockchain concepts: smart contracts, cryptocurrencies, decentralized finance (DeFi), NFTs, and more.
* Develop practical projects and prototypes that solve real-world problems.
* Organize workshops, seminars, and hackathons for knowledge sharing.
* Create partnerships with industry professionals and other universities.
* Encourage research and publication in blockchain technology.

---

## Core Areas of Focus

* **Blockchain Fundamentals** – Cryptography, consensus mechanisms, distributed ledgers
* **Smart Contracts** – Ethereum, Solidity, smart contract development
* **Decentralized Applications (dApps)** – Designing and deploying real-world applications
* **Cryptocurrencies** – Mining, wallets, and crypto-economics
* **NFTs & Metaverse** – Exploring digital assets and blockchain in virtual worlds
* **DeFi** – Lending, staking, decentralized exchanges
* **Blockchain Security** – Threat analysis, secure coding, and auditing

---

## Membership

Membership is open to **all Mbarara University students** interested in blockchain technology.

**Member Roles**:

* **Core Team** – Leads projects, organizes workshops, manages group activities
* **Members** – Participate in projects, discussions, and learning activities
* **Research Contributors** – Focus on research papers, blockchain articles, and development
